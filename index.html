Neighbourhood walkability research

Perceived Walkability Survey

We want to understand how different aspects of your neighbourhood encourage or discourage your walking.

  • Time commitment: Please set aside about 15 to 20 minutes to complete this survey.
  • Your perspectives: We are interested in understanding your perception of various neighbourhood aspects and whether they encourage or discourage your walking behaviour.
  • Facilities, destinations, situations and places. For these items, in addition to your perception rating, we will ask you to identify where you usually experience them, — for example, up to a 10-minute walk or an 11–20 minute walk from your residence.
  • Neighbourhood conditions: For theses conditions, in addition to your perception rating, we will simply ask you to indicate whether or not this aspect exists in your neighbourhood.
  • Defining access: When evaluating your access to services or facilities, please think beyond just the closest facility. Consider whether a place is truly usable for you — by taking into account factors such as affordability and your personal preferences. The one that is usable for you is the one you should be considering while answering the relevant question(s).
  • Changed your mind? At any time before you submit, you can use the Back button to return to an earlier section and correct your answers.

The survey moves through six sections. You can download a copy of your answers as a PDF at the end.
